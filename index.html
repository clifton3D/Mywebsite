A Little About Me

Hi!

I was born on the 14th of October 1989. I walked down a long and entwined path to get to where I am today. Still not where I want to be, I move forward, and strive to overcome any of my hardships. I have had to learn many hard lessons, often times placing them directly in front of myself. I work hard and am loyal to almost a fault.
My dream was to become a teacher, mentor and an inspiration to others.

This is my platform to be creative, to fulfill my dreams, and show that we should never give up on our dreams.
